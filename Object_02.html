Brain

This is a self portrait of myself in the year 2020. I like to make self portraits once in a while to learn more about my perspective on the current state of existence. A lot of my life was spent online this year because of the inability to have face to face interaction, so I decided to make a digital portrait.

The faces were scanned and created using 3D Face Reconstruction from a Single Image https://cvl-demos.cs.nott.ac.uk/vrn/.

GLTF in Blender.
35mb 2020

Audio: Cloud- Turning, A song chosen to represent self.
