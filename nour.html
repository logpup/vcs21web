Nour (they/he) is an Egyptian-Muslim digital illustrator and animator. His work under the title ‘EYE SEE’ samples different subjects which dabbles into thema such as stylized devils from Biblical lore, cyberpunk magicians, and a story of his in-the-works which deals with the Void.
